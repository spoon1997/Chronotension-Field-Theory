Quantum Chronotension Field Theory (QCFT)

Abstract

QCFT is a proposed Theory of Everything that replaces spacetime geometry with a dynamic field of time viscosity, denoted by η(x,t). All matter, forces, and cosmological structure arise from gradients and topological knots within this field. Chronodes encode particles, Gradia replaces gravity, and redshift arises from viscosity decay — not expansion. The theory is renormalizable, predictive, and background-independent, unifying quantum mechanics and gravity without invoking dark energy or singularities.

Implications

  • Redefines redshift, lensing, structure formation, and gravity
  • Explains consciousness as coherent η-gap structure
  • Replaces particles with field solitons (chronodes)
  • Predicts field-based technological possibilities
  • Fully falsifiable via clock drift, redshift anomalies, and η-wave detection
