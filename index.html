🌌 Chronotension Field Theory (CFT)

🧠 What Is CFT?

Chronotension Field Theory (CFT) is a unified model of time and gravity. It proposes that both arise from variations in a continuous field of temporal resistance, denoted η(x,t).

In this framework:

  • Redshift is caused by gradients in η(x,t), not spatial expansion.
  • Gravity is the curvature of time flow, not spacetime geometry.
  • The universe is not expanding — it is unfurling through progressive relaxation of the time-field.

This reinterpretation challenges standard assumptions and reproduces key cosmological observations through a resistive field, rather than through mass-energy curvature or inflation.
